How to submit a reimbursement request? Click 'Reimbursement Request', click 'New', fill information and save. Click 'Items' for adding items. Click Files for uploading supporting files. Finnaly click 'Alert' for sending an alert email to your manager for approving.

How to approve a reimbursement request? Click 'Reimbursement Request', find the request, click 'Approving', tick the check box and save. Finnaly click 'Alert' for sending an alert email to an accountant for processing.

How to process a reimbursement request? Click 'Reimbursement Request', find the request, click 'Processing', tick the check box and save. Finnaly click 'Alert' for sending an alert email to the requester.
